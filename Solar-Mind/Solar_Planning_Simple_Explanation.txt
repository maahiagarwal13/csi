Problem Statement: Intelligent Solar Feasibility and Planning System for Buildings
(Simplified Explanation)

The Problem:
Buildings use a massive amount of electricity. Many building owners want to switch to solar power, but making that decision is confusing and complicated. Right now, to figure out how many solar panels you need, how much space they will take up, how much it will cost, and how much money you will save, you usually have to hire an expert. Because electricity usage changes throughout the day and year, it's very hard to guess the perfect solar setup on your own.

The Solution:
We need to build a smart, data-driven software platform that does all this complicated math automatically. By simply looking at a building's past electricity data, this system will design the perfect solar installation plan for that specific building.

Key Features of the System:
1. Automatic Data Analysis: It will automatically read and understand the building's past electricity usage records.
2. AI-Powered Sizing: It will use Artificial Intelligence to calculate exactly how much solar capacity is needed to replace grid electricity.
3. Space Estimation: It will figure out how much roof or land space is required to fit the recommended solar panels.
4. Cost & Savings Calculator: It will estimate the total cost of the project (installation + maintenance) and calculate the "payback period" (how long it takes for the energy savings to pay for the system).
5. Weather Integration: It will use local sunlight and geography data to accurately predict how much power the panels will actually generate.
6. User-Friendly Dashboard: It will present all this complex data, projected savings, and costs in clean, interactive, and easy-to-read charts and graphs.

The Ultimate Goal:
To provide an easy-to-use tool that takes the guesswork out of going solar. This will encourage and empower more building owners, institutions, and policymakers to confidently adopt renewable energy.
